Mathematics: Profit Projection

Unlike the english IPBA, the math one was a little it trickier. Us students were given
a profit projection to complete about a make-believe puppet show.

The profit projection was divided into 3 parts. In the first part, we had to determine
the profit, and also determine the shares of the organizers and the producers. On
the second part, our group had to decide on our ticket price and on the other things
needed to complete this. We also had to apply google sheet functions while doing so.
Meanwhile for the third part, we had to justify our chosen ticket price and why we
think it would be logical for both the audience and the producers and organizers.

Filipino & Science

The given intructions regarding the Filipino IPBA was to simply write a "talata" with
15 or more sentences.

A talata has 3 parts, just like an english essay: the introduction, the body, and the
conclusion.

This IPBA is also connected to science and social studies. We had to write about
the connections of the laws of newton to things that the ancient Chinese civilization
